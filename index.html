About Ash Shabab Ruh Ul Ummah

Our Mission

Ash Shabab Ruh Ul Ummah is dedicated to the upliftment and protection of the Ummah worldwide. In these critical times, we focus our efforts on the humanitarian crisis in Palestine, where our brothers and sisters face unimaginable hardships.

We believe in practical solutions combined with spiritual support to help those in need, following the principles of compassion and justice in Islam.

Palestine Humanitarian Crisis

2023 - Present

Starvation as a Weapon

Over 2.3 million Palestinians in Gaza are facing severe food insecurity, with 90% regularly skipping meals. Children are suffering from acute malnutrition at alarming rates.

Ongoing

Medical Catastrophe

Hospitals are overwhelmed with limited supplies. Over 40,000 injured civilians lack proper medical care due to blockade restrictions on medical supplies.

Daily Reality

Psychological Trauma

An entire generation of Palestinian children is growing up with severe PTSD from constant bombardment, loss of family members, and daily survival struggles.
